    Arborec¶

    Note

    Absol mod references accurate to Async TI4 as of 14 Oct 24

    • Home System¶

      • Nestphar (3/2)
      • Commodities: 3

    • Starting Space Units¶

      • 1 Carrier
      • 1 Cruiser
      • 2 Fighters

    • Starting Ground Units¶

      • 4 Infantry
      • 1 Space Dock
      • 1 PDS

    • Starting Technologies¶

      Magen Defense Grid Ω¶

      Requirements:

      At the start of ground combat on a planet that contains 1 or more of your structures, you may produce 1 hit and assign it to 1 of your opponent's ground forces.

      Magen Defense Grid¶

      Requirements:

      At the start of ground combat on a planet that contains 1 or more of your structures, you may produce 1 hit per structure and assign them to your opponent's ground forces.

      After you perform a tactical action, you may exhaust this card and spend 4 resources to place a structure on a planet you control in the active system.

    • Faction Abilities¶

      Mitosis¶

      Your space docks cannot produce infantry.

      At the start of the status phase, place 1 infantry from your reinforcements on any planet you control.

    • Flagship¶

      Duha Menaimon¶

      Unit Type: Flagship

      Cost 8 | Combat 7(x2) | Move 1 | Capacity 5

      Sustain Damage

      After you activate this system, you may produce up to 5 units in this system.

    • Mech¶

      Letani Behemoth¶

      Unit Type: Mech

      Cost 2 | Combat 6

      Planetary Shield | Sustain Damage | Production 2

      Deploy: When you would use your Mitosis faction ability you may replace 1 of your infantry with 1 mech from your reinforcements instead.

      Letani Behemoth¶

      Unit Type: Mech

      Cost 2 | Combat 6

      Planetary Shield | Sustain Damage | Production 3

      This unit may count as a structure when it is on a planet.

      Deploy: When you would place a structure on a planet, you may place 1 mech on that planet instead.

    • Special Units¶

      Letani Warrior I¶

      Unit Type: Infantry

      Cost 1(x2) | Combat 8

      Production 1

    • Faction Technologies¶

      Letani Warrior II¶

      Requirements:

      Cost 1(x2) | Combat 7

      Production 2.

      After this unit is destroyed, roll 1 die. If the result is 6 or greater, place the unit on this card.

      At the start of your next turn, place each unit that is on this card on a planet you control in your home.

      Bioplasmosis¶

      Requirements:

      At the end of the status phase, you may remove any number of infantry from planets you control and place them on 1 or more planets you control in the same or adjacent systems.

      Letani Warrior II¶

      Requirements:

      Cost 1(x2) | Combat 7

      Production 2

      After this unit is destroyed, place the unit on this card.

      At the start of your next turn, for every 2 units that are on this card, place 1 on a planet you control and return the rest to your reinforcements.

      Bioplasmosis¶

      Requirements:

      When you produce units in a system, you may exhaust this card and reduce the combined Production value of your units in that system by up to half (rounded down) to reduce the combined cost of the produced units by the same amount.

    • Leaders¶

      Agent: Letani Ospha - Unity Through Peace¶

      Unlock: Always Unlocked

      Action:

      Exhaust this card and choose a player's non-fighter ship: that player may replace that ship with one from their reinforcements that costs up to 2 more than the replaced ship.

      Commander: Dirzuga Rophal - Peace Through Strength¶

      Unlock: Have 12 ground forces on planets you control.

      After another player activates a system that contains 1 or more of your units that have Production:

      You may produce 1 unit in that system.

      Hero: Letani Miasmiala - Strength Through Unity¶

      Unlock: Have 3 scored objectives.

      Ultrasonic Emitter: Overgrowth

      Action:

      Produce any number of units in any number of systems that contain 1 or more of your ground forces. Then, purge this card.

    • Faction Promissory Note¶

      Arborec - Stymie Ω¶

      After another player moves ships into a system that contains 1 or more of your units:

      You may place 1 command token from that player's reinforcements in any non-home system.

      Then, return this card to the Arborec player.
