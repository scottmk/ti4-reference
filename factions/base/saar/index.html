      Antimass Deflectors

      Requirements: None

      Your ships can move into and through asteroid fields.

      When other players' units use Space Cannon against your units, apply -1 to the result of each die roll.

      Antimass Deflectors

      Requirements: None

      Your ships can move into and through asteroid fields and retain their printed Move value when moving out of nebulae. When other players' units use Space Cannon against your units, apply -1 to the result of each die roll.

    • Leaders

      Agent: Captain Mendosa Captain of the Fifth Fleet

      Unlock: Always Unlocked

      When a player activates a system:

      You may exhaust this card to increase the move value of 1 of that player's ships to match the move value of the ship on the game board that has the highest move value.

      Commander: Rowl Sarring Relay Engineer

      Unlock: Have 3 space docks on the game board.

      When you produce fighters or infantry:

      You may place each of those units at any of your space docks that are not blockaded.

      Hero: Gurno Aggero Captain of the First Fleet

      Unlock: Have 3 scored objectives.

      Armageddon Relay: The Lay of Lisis

      Action:

      Choose 1 system that is adjacent to 1 of your space docks. Destroy all other players' infantry and fighters in that system. Then, purge this card.

    • Breakthrough

      Deorbit Barrage

      Action: Exhaust this card and spend any amount of resources to choose a planet up to 2 systems away from an asteroid field that contains your ships; roll a number of dice equal to the amount spent and assign 1 hit to a ground force on that planet for each roll of 4 or greater.

    • Faction Promissory Note

      Clan of Saar Ragh's Call

      After you commit 1 or more units to land on a planet:

      Remove all of the Saar player's ground forces from that planet and place them on a planet controlled by the Saar player.

      Then return this card to the Saar player.
