    Salient Sun - Alliance (Liberation of Ordinian) ¶

    • Special Setup¶

      The Salient Sun joint task force is an alliance of the Federation of Sol and the Xxcha Kingdom for the Liberation of Ordinian scenario found in Codex IV.

      The Xxcha Kingdom and Federation of Sol players are allied; they resolve the setup step 3 from the Alliance game variant in Codex II. They also gain the FSS Orlando hero card. Both the Xxcha and Sol player must agree in order to use the FSS Orlando.

      Within this alliance, the game ends when one player has 12 victory points and the other has 10 victory points.

      Hero: F.S.S. Orlando – Apollo Protocol¶

      Unlock: The Nekro Virus has 5 victory points.

      Intelligence Unshackled

      When one of your units in the Ordinian system is destroyed:

      You may purge this card to designate that unit as the catalyst; roll 1 die for each other player's unit in the system. For each result equal to or greater than the catalyst's combat value, destroy that unit.

      "Seek shelter." The command echoed in their minds. As if to illustrate the point, the air around them resonated with a hum, then a pulse, then a scream of deadly intensity.
