    Faction Technologies¶

    Whenever you take part in a slice—including the inaugural splice—you may instead choose to take one of the following faction technologies instead of a splice card. These technologies do not have any prerequisites, do not count as any technology color, and cannot be lost once gained.

    Note that you must play with the Prophecy of Kings expansion to use the abilities preceded with a icon.

    • Wavelength ¶

      Your ships can move into and through asteroid fields.

      When you activate a system, you may explore 1 planet in that system that contains 1 or more of your units.

    • Antimatter ¶

      Your ships can retreat into adjacent systems that do not contain other players' units, even if you do not have units or control planets in that system.

      After you perform a tactical action in a system that contains a frontier token, if you have 1 or more ships in that system, explore that token.
